Why Trust Us?

Community-Driven

We're part of the metal community, not corporate lawyers looking to profit from your success. We genuinely want to see artists protected from exploitation.

Real Experience

Our review team includes artists who've signed good deals, bad deals, and everything in between. We know what to look for because we've lived it.

No Agenda

We don't represent labels, we don't sell legal services, and we're not trying to recruit you. Our only goal is fair treatment for artists.

Nonprofit Mission

As a registered nonprofit, we're legally bound to serve the community, not shareholders. This service will always be free.

Common Questions

Is this really free?

Yes, completely free. No hidden fees, no upsells, no obligations. This is our contribution to protecting artists in the metal community.

How anonymous is it really?

We never ask for identifying information. You can use a burner email, Discord alt, or any anonymous contact method. Our team reviews the contract without knowing who you are or which label it's from.

Can you provide legal advice?

We provide educational feedback and highlight concerning clauses, but we're not lawyers and this isn't legal advice. For legal representation, you'll need to consult a lawyer licensed in your jurisdiction.

How long does the review take?

Typically 5-7 business days. Complex contracts or high volume may take longer, but we'll always get back to you with a thorough analysis.

What if I've already signed?

We can still review it and help you understand what you've committed to. Depending on the terms and your jurisdiction, there may be options for renegotiation or early termination.
